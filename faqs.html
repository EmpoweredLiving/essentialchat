Is Essential Chats safe to use for medical advice?

Essential Chats is designed for educational and informational purposes only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical advice or treatment.

How accurate are the answers I get from Essential Chats?

The AI gives helpful information, but it’s not perfect. Always double-check what it says to make sure it fits your needs. Think of the answers as a starting point, and feel free to adjust or personalize them to work best for you.

Also, sometimes it may repeat itself or pause before continuing. If that happens, you can click continue or rephrase your question to be more specific. If it doesn't give the answer you're looking for, try asking in a clearer or more direct way.

How do I save a chat to my computer or a word document?

In the chat window, type: Save chat.
Essential Chats will generate a link that you can click on and download your session as a document.

I got a token error when chatting! What happened?

If you ever see a red error message about rate limits or tokens, don't worry! That just means the system is catching its breath from a long response. Just wait 2–3 seconds and type "continue" — your question wasn’t lost.

How do I change my subscription?

In order to change your subscription, you should first cancel your existing subscription. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'. Once you've completed this task, Click 'Subscribe' in the the left menu and choose the new plan you want to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime. When you cancel, you will no longer be able to chat so we suggest cancelling towards the end of your paid month/year to get the most out of Essential Chats. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'.
